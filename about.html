About The Sandbagger’s Tour

The Sandbagger’s Tour is a golf lifestyle brand built around a familiar character: the player who’s “never broken 90”… until there’s cash on the line.

We’re creating apparel and golf accessories with tour-style branding, classic golf vibes, and sayings that golfers instantly recognize.

What we’ll release

  • Golf apparel: hats, polos, tees, hoodies
  • Golf gear: towels, ball markers, tees, accessories
  • Artwork + sayings inspired by “sandbagger culture”

Down the road: social content, community stories, and possibly tournaments honoring sandbaggers everywhere.
